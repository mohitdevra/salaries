Data Engineer Salary

Data Analyst Salary

Data Scientist v/s Data Engineer Salary

So we can see here that till 2021 the median salary of data engineer was more than the median salary of a data scientist, But between 2021-2022 the median salary of data scientist has grown exponentially and surpassed the median salary of a data scientist.

we have also saw a fall in both data scientist and data engineer median salaries in year 2024, But the fall in data engineer median salary is less than the fall in data scientist salary.
